Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

The Perfect Combination of Chocolate and Peanut Butter

Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are a classic candy loved by millions around the world. The smooth peanut butter center enveloped in a rich chocolate shell is a flavor pairing that's simply irresistible.

Fun Facts about Reese's

  • Reese's Peanut Butter Cups were first introduced in 1928 by Harry Burnett "H.B." Reese, a former dairy farmer and shipping foreman for the Hershey Company.
  • Each year, enough Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are produced to circle the Earth 1.5 times.
  • Reese's is the best-selling brand of peanut butter cups in the United States.
  • The Reese's brand was acquired by The Hershey Company in 1963.
